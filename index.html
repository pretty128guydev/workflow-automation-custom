How it works?
Automation Setup
Set up your automation in three steps: select a trigger, connect your apps, and create natural language instructions. AI provides all the tools you need for integration and automation.
Customer Engagement
AI ensures instant 24/7 support, responds to customers in seconds, integrates into your website, and uses knowledge bases to provide accurate answers.
Lead and Sales Management
AI helps you find and qualify leads, automate outreach, prepare sales documents, and set follow-up reminders for improved sales efficiency.
Advanced Support Features
AI collects conversation data, shares key quotes with your team, answers Slack questions, and keeps your knowledge base updated in real time.
Email Optimization
Save time with automated email sorting, draft creation, and inbox organization. AI simplifies email management for you.
Meeting Assistant
AI records meetings, takes notes, suggests time slots for scheduling, and provides feedback to improve your discussions.
Scaling and Data Management 
AI automates calls, communicates with customers in 50+ languages, processes large data sets, and supports multiple accounts from the same service.
AI Model Configuration 
Choose AI models that suit your tasks best and manage processes at any stage. Lindy supports collaboration among multiple agents.
Security and Reliability
AI ensures enterprise-grade security and compliance with the highest data protection standards.
